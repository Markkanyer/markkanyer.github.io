About Me

As a Seattleite born just south of the city, I developed a deep passion for creativity, technology, strategy, and storytelling as a young child. Even as a kid, I was a walking billboard, always wearing Mountain Dew and Dr. Pepper logos on my t-shirts without realizing I was already marketing.   

Marketing became a clear career path after I ended up in a marketing class in high school. I quickly realized that marketing combined creativity, strategy, and technology in a way that just felt completely natural to me. (And with Mark as my middle name, I figured it just made sense.)

Outside of marketing, I enjoy staying active through swimming, weightlifting and hiking, and I love traveling to discover new and exciting places. In my downtime, I immerse myself in video games, movies, and music, and I value spending quality time with friends and family.
